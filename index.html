The Immigration Intake Bottleneck

Preparing petition packages manually wastes up to 50% of an immigration firm's billable hours.

🔍

Chasing Documents

Clients struggle to provide exact civil certificates, tax transcripts, or translation proofs. Following up takes weeks of email ping-pong.

⚠️

RFE & Rejection Risks

Lacking sufficient evidence weight (such as weak bona fide marriage records) or using incorrect form editions triggers devastating RFEs.

✍️

Manual Fact Mapping

Paralegals manually copy-paste names, addresses, and entry dates across Form I-130, I-485, and I-864, risking formatting and transcribing errors.

The AI-Native Petition Assembly Engine

MeritX bridges the gap between messy client consultations and polished USCIS petition packages.

1. Case Playbooks & Evidence Mapping

Upload consultation audio or case files. Our engine identifies the case scenario (e.g. USC Petitioner with Adjustment of Status) and extracts required facts, mapping evidence directly to USCIS eligibility criteria.

  • Dynamic Fact Extraction: Captures lawful entry records, tax filings, and prior marriages.
  • Evidence Mapping: Automatically classifies files (e.g. lease -> Cohabitation evidence).
  • Auditable Citations: Every extracted fact traces back to timestamps in audio or pages in PDFs.

2. Completeness Scoring & Red Flag Checks

No more manual file auditing. MeritX's deterministic check layer evaluates evidence counts and flags inadmissibility bars, calculating a clear completeness breakdown.

  • Completeness Score: Four-dimension breakdown showing exact case readiness.
  • Deterministic Rule Flags: Detects overstays, date conflicts, and financial gaps.
  • Actionable Feedback: Instantly alerts if evidence falls below USCIS guidelines.

3. Form Mapping & Package Assembly

Generate attorney-ready outputs in minutes. The engine maps facts directly to USCIS forms, builds the cover letter, and structures the evidence binder.

  • Form Field Mapping: Maps intake facts into Form I-130, I-130A, I-485, and I-864 schema.
  • Petition Cover Letter: Drafts letterheads with case facts and legal criteria references.
  • Filing Exhibit Index: Auto-numbers exhibits (A to N) mapped to eligibility categories.

Supported Case Playbooks

MeritX operates on deterministic legal configurations. Custom playbooks are compiled to your firm's standards.
